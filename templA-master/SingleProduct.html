The Power of Habit

In ‘The Power of Habit’, award-winning New York Times business
reporter Charles Duhigg takes us to the thrilling edge of scientific
discoveries that explain why habits exist and how they can be
changed.
With an ability to distill vast amounts of information into engrossing
narratives, Duhigg brings to life a whole new understanding of
human nature and its potential for transformation. Along the way we
learn why some companies struggle to change, despite years of
trying, while others remake themselves overnight.
